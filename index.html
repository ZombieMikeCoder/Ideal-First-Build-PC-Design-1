Welcome To Ideal First Build PC

This website provides some basic information on what sort of components and software are needed in order to build a fully functioning PC of your own.


This Site is intended to help guide anyone who would like to build a PC of their own but has little knowledge of PC building. This site goes through the preparation needed before even starting the build to getting Windows installed/running.


Note - The images used throughout this site are both my mine and what I have saved from various locations on the internet, as this site is only for educational purposes and no money shall be made from this site then copyrighted material should not be taken into account.


Note - The Information on this site is not updated regularly therefore new components and software from the same manufacture maybe available. It is advised to check the official manufactures website for updated products.


Note - The information and recommendations given are from my own personal experiences with building PCs and I am in no way affiliated or sponsored by any of the manufactures.
